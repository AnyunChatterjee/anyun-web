hi! my name is pronounced "ah-noon."

i'm a phd student at the klein college of media and communication and researcher at policylab.

i earned a bachelors in biochemistry from berkeley and a masters in environmental health from george washington.



before returning to academia, i spent 6 years building health and political communication campaigns for nonprofits and governments.

i developed my research agenda by observing how people's behavior changed as i placed ads and psas in front of them......

......sometimes they believed the content and said it was important, but didn't change their behavior.

......other times my content made people mad and they would react with harsh words and rile up their friends.

......and in some rare instances, the people i reached would actually take action and go do the thing i wanted them to do.

i'm trying to better understand this behavior using ideas from social psychology, media studies, and public health.



i also enjoy teaching and getting people excited about the subjects i love, like health communication, rhetoric, and media analysis.
