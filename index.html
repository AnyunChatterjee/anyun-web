hi! my name is pronounced "ah-noon."

i'm a phd student at the klein college of media and communication and researcher at policylab.

i have a bachelors degree in biochemistry from uc berkeley and a masters degree in environmental health from george washington.

i built media/communications campaigns for nonprofits, governmental organizations, and health agencies for about 6 years before returning to academia.

during that time, i became fascinated in the questions of why we behave the way we do, and how messages impact us individually and as groups.

now, my research agenda straddles three domains: human behavior, media & rhetoric, and public health.

lately i've been thinking about how to use webapps and other digital platforms to increase physical activity, voting, and other pro-public health/pro-civic engagement outcomes.

i also enjoy teaching and getting people excited about the subjects i love.

i've been teaching for about a decade now, in topics ranging from biostatistics, to media studies, to topics in public heatlh.
